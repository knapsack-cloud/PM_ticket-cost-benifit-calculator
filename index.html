Cost / Benifit Calculator

A simple calculator for determining the feasibility score of a feature or enhancement.

Benifits

How much does this benefit the user directly? Are user's requesting the feature, has data shown it will improve their experience?

1: Little to no value, likely makes our lives easier rather than the user's.

5: A welcomed and appreciated feature, not necessarily required.

10: Absolutely critical to the value proposition. Wouldn’t use the product without it.

1: Likely improves the user experience a bit, but doesn’t set us apart in the market at all.

5: Provides a notable advantage in the market.

10: Required to be noticeable in the market or even considered against competitors.

Costs

1: trivial. Could go in a patch release.

5: Requires a decent amount of time, would go in a minor release.

10: Requires major underlying restructuring or refactor, would result in a major version bump.
